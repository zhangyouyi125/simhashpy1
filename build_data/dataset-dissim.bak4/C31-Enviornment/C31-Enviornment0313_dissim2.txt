Continuous determination of trace cadmium，arsenic，copper and lead in environmental water by stabilized temperature platform furnace AAS using longitudinal arrangement Zeemen-effect background correction. Wu Hua，Wu Fuquan，Li Shaonan (Suzhou Research Institute of Environmental Science , Suzhou 215004).China Environmental Science.1998,18(4):374～377
Abstract― A new method for the precise determination of trace cadmium, arsenic, copper and lead in environmental water by using longitudinal arrangement Zeemen-effect background correction, maximum power heating, peak area integration and Pd as a matrix modified,called altogether the stabilized temperature platform furnace (STPF) AAS,has been studied. This method is rapid, accurate and precise. The detection limits(3σ) , relative standard deviation(n=6) and recovery for Cd,As,Cu and Pb were 2.5×10-13，5.0×10-11,3.8×10-11 and,2.5×10-11g;3.4％～4.9％,2.5％～4.3％,1.8％～3.6％ and 1.8％～4.1％;93.5％～106％,91.5％～103％,93.5％～110％ and 91.8%～108％respectively. This satisfactory method has shown an outlook for the absolute analysis.
Key words:stabilized temperature platform furnace 　Zeemen-effect　Cd、As、Cu、Pb　environmental water
　　为评价环境水质质量需要监测的项目，诸如痕量元素镉、砷、铜和铅，由于水体易受环境影响，其基体与含量往往变化不一，靠单一的方法测定难以准确定量。主要从事原子光谱分析。佛蒙特州歇业公司百分比最高，为67%，在调查的12家公司中有8家歇业。
　　研究人员Jamie Anderson说，虽然受影响的大多数是雇员少于25人的公司，但涉及面较广，且大多数公司已从业7年以上。他说：“这就表明整个环保业都受到影响”。调查对象包括咨询公司、环境研究所、治理商、供应商、土壤钻探服务公司和监测井钻探公司等。
