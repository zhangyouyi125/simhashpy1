中外优秀男子体操运动员全能成绩的结构特征
刘卫
　　摘要：以偏相关分析和灰色关联分析为主，对近10年间奥运会和世界体操锦标赛两大赛，中外优秀男子运动员的成绩结构进行统计分析，认为：双杠、鞍马、单杠对世界优秀运动员的全能成绩的偏相关度最大，其次为吊环、自由体操、跳马，6个子项之间，自由体操与鞍马、吊环与双杠、跳马与自由体操、单杠与吊环的偏相关度最高。中国队队员的全能成绩结构与世界优秀选手的特点有差别。
　　关键词：男子体操；全能；子项；相关

Constructional feature of all-round results in Chinese and foreign men gymnastices
LIU Wei
（Department of PE，teachers’college of Qindao university，Qindao 266071，China）
　　Abstract：According to the theory of the partial interrelation and grey interrelation，We analyzed the Olympics and the world gymnastic championships which took place in the  recent ten years：we also analyzed the statistics of the Chinese foreign all-round gymnastics athelets’results．We believed：the partial interrelation of the parallel bars，horizontal bar and saddle-horse to the all-round resatts of the world excellent athletes are the highest，the next are therings，free exercises and vaulting-horse．Among these six individual event-free exercise and saddle-horse，rings and  parallel bars，vaulting-horse and free evercises，horizontal bar and rings，are the highest．The construction of the Chinese players’all-round results is different to the world excellent players’features．
　　Keywords：men gymnastics；all-round；individual event；interrelation
1　前言
　　中国男子体操队于1995年重夺团体冠军，并在此后的几年中一直处于霸主地位，然而未能看到各子项全面开花的局面。因此，有必要重新认识当前世界优秀男子体操运动员全能成绩的结构特点，为今后我国各级专业运动队充分利用各子项之间的相互作用，协调各子项训练比重，提高训练效益提供部分参考依据。

Development and Prospects of the National Fitness Program in China
-As Seen from the Development Tendencies of Sport for All in the World
Xiong Douyin
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　1998，18(2):3
(National Research Institute of Sports Science, Beijing, China 100061)
Abstract The function of sport in modem society, with background of the rise of sport for all in the world, was 
discussed and eight major tendencies of the development of sport for all were summarized based on the latest 
information, in the purposes to provide experience and examples for the implementation of the National Fitness 
Program in China. After introduction of the background and characteristics of the National Fitness Program, the 
author concluded that: the implementation of the National Fitness Program in China has played an example for 
developing countries; it marked a new stage in China's reform and opening to the outside world and construction 
for modernization; and it has showed a well-coordinated development of sport in China. Problems in implementing 
the Program were also pointed out by the author as compared with developed countries.
Key words sport for all, National Fitness Program, trend analysis, prospective study, China, international
1 国际大众体育兴起的社会背景
　　近半个世纪以来，以微电子技术为核心的信息技术迅速发展，一批高技术、新技术、新材
料、新能源以及生物工程的应用，正把人类从工业社会推向所谓“第三次浪潮”的信息社会。鞍马的内容包括各种摆越、全旋、转体、移位和倒立等动作，尤其以各种全旋动作构成其最为壮观的技术特点。
　　近些年来在各种世界大赛中，各国高水平自由体操运动员的“灵魂性”动作（跟头、跟头串等）的数量、难度及完成情况相差无几，因而整套动作的编排是否新颖、独特，就成为运动员和教练员追求的另一目标，也是裁判员评判整套动作的重要标准。鞍马的全旋动作出现在自由体操比赛中已有10几年的历史，从最早的单腿全旋、双腿全旋、托马斯全旋，发展到托马斯全旋打滚、托马斯全旋成倒立及转体再接托马斯全旋的高难度连接，使自由体操的编排更具特色。此外，做为一名优秀自由体操运动员所俱备的身体素质，如动作速度、速度耐力、爆发力、协调、灵敏素质及良好的前庭分析功能等等，都对熟练掌握鞍马动作技术有很大的促进作用。(3)实验研究方法：以1995～1996年度为
