美国将发射卫星监测地球环境
　　和美国海军签订的一项协议内容是开发和运行一颗新的卫星，它将提供图象，便于进行环境评价并监测人类活动或自然现象引起的环境变化。
　　1994年起弗吉尼亚州Arlington的空间技术开发公司(STDC)从事于将国家研究所与空间有关的技术商业化，将在2000年年中发射该新卫星。该卫星名为海军地球地图观察者(NEMO)，将其放置于近极地的低地球轨道，使它能至少每7d观察到地球每一点。
关键词　长波紫外线　致突变作用　DNA氧化损伤
Mutagenesis of M13mp2 phage by near-ultraviolet light. Hao Weidong,Shang Lanqin,Zheng Yu,Jin Qingzhong (School of Public Health, Beijing Medical University, Beijing 100083).China Environmental Science.1998,18(5):472～475
Abstract―The mutagenic potential of UVA (ultraviolet A) on the lacZα region of M13mp2 phage was observed in wild-type CSH50 and mutM-deficient (MF67) E.coli.The results showed that UVA induced mutations at lacZα region of phage and the mutation frequency was significantly higher in MF67 than in CSH50. The effect of free radical scavanger (mannitol) on mutagenesis by UVA was also investigated. The mutagenesis was inhibited in part by 0.1mol/L of mannitol. These results indicate that UVA can cause mutation in phage and its mutagenic effect can be ascribed to oxidative damage of DNA.
Key words:ultraviolet A　mutagenesis　DNA oxidative damage
