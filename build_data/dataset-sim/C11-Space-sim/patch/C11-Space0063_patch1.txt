拟合激光陀螺数学模型的方法研究
杨　恒啊〕隆∶鳌∏赜涝　王建培
Frank*Bosselman
摘　要　在分析激光陀螺的数学模型和三次样条函数拟合原理的基础上，针对俄罗斯某型低精度激光陀螺实际输入-输出特性曲线的数据，应用三次样条拟合方法进行分析处理，推导出了适用于非线性工作范围误差补偿且符合实际陀螺特性的数学模型。尽管不同类型的卫星大角度机动对控制的要求有所不同,但迅速抑制挠性附件因机动而激发的振动以保持机动过程平稳,以及在机动末端不产生大超调并迅速实现高精度姿态定位,是挠性卫星大角度机动控制的两个基本要求。
关键词　三次样条拟合原理　曲线拟合　激光陀螺(环形激光器)　数学模型
A Study on Fitting Ring Laser Gyro Mathematic Model
Yang Heng　Frank.Bosselman　Chen Ming　Qin Yongyuan　Wang Jianpei
　　Abstract　Based on the analysis of mathematic model of ring laser gyro (RLG) and the principle of cubic spline data interpolation,pointing to the data of practical input-output characteristic curve of some Russian model of RLG and making analysis and treatment with the cubic spline data interpolation method,the mathematic model applying to error compensation in the nonlinear working range and being accordance with the practical gyro characteristic has been deduced.The method proposed in this paper can be applied to accuracy compensation of the RLG with worse linearity and lower working accuracy in the working range.
　　Key words　Principle of cubic spline data interpolation,Curve fit,Ring laser gyro,Mathematic model

ANALYTIC PREDICTION OF CUTTING 
TEMPERATURE FOR HARD PROCESSING MATERIALS
Lui Weina
(Chang chun Institute of Optics and Fine Mechanics．Changchu．130022)
Yang Lifeng
(The Northeast Surveying and Designing Institute．Changchu．130021)
　　Abstract　Based on heat transfer,by useing a finite difference numerical method and personal computer,temperature field at cutting area in two dimensional machining process ispredicted.Take machining titanium alloy by useing diamond tool and carbide tool for examples,the cutting temperature is calculated.The calculated temperature is in good agreement with that measured.This indicates that computer simulation of cutting temperature is applicable.It also provides a new analytic method for the study of cutting and processing features of hard processing materials.A large amount of experiments will be saved thus.It lay a fundation for predictingthe optimum cutting process and instructing the development of new cutter materials.
　　Key words　Titanium alloy Cutting temperature Analytic prediction Finite difference
1　引言
　　随着宇航事业的迅速发展,配备于各类高新仪器与装备的高质量、性能好的材料不断问世,这给加工与制造技术提出了更新、更高的要求。本文提出了符合三次样条拟合原理的多项式拟合某型激光陀螺数学模型，旨在对工作区线性误差较大的激光陀螺进行误差补偿的方法予以研究。
1　激光陀螺的理想输出模型
　　理想情况下，当环形激光器在惯性空间旋转时，具有不同辐值、相位和偏振特性的相向行波将呈现非交互效应。这时，激光陀螺的输入―输出特性为图1中直线1，它通过坐标原点，与横坐标轴之间夹角的正切等于比例系数k，因而，陀螺的理想数学模型是斜率为k的一条直线。在实际情况中，激光陀螺的输入―输出模型曲线(如图1中曲线2)存在着闭锁区，也就是说激光器转速小于闭锁阈区时就感测不出角速度的变化。

2　样条的概念和三次样条函数的表达式
