基于卫星质保体系的神经网络模型
舒适　 陆力斌　 朱鸿昌　 
摘　要：针对质保体系基础上的卫星产品保证管理，应用多层前馈神经网络原理和方法，建立了基于无卫星产品保证要求的质保体系与基于卫星产品保证要求的质保体系神经网络模型，以用神经网络模型来模拟和控制卫星产品的质量水平；并用模型比较了两种质保体系的联系与区别。
关键词：产品保证；质保体系；神经网络；模型
分类号：472.2　文献标识码：A
文章编号：1006－1630（2001）01－0022－05
The Neural Networks Model of Satellite Quality Assurance System
SHU Shi（Management College, Harbin Institute of Technology，Heilongjiang Harbin 150001， China）　
LU Li-bin（Management College, Harbin Institute of Technology，Heilongjiang Harbin 150001， China）　
ZHU Hong-chang（No. 509 Institute of SAST, Shanghai 200240, China）　

Abstract：The Neural Networks models of quality assurance system without satellite product assurance and quality assurance system with satellite product assurance are set up by use of theory and method of Neural Networks to simulate and control the satellite quality degree. And we also compare the difference and the relationships of these two systems by means of Neural Networks models.
Keywords：Product assurance；Quality assurance system；Neural networks; Model
