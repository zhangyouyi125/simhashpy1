基于图像自匹配性的景象匹配区的选定准则
马龙　 缪栋　 沈绪榜　 
摘　要：定义了图像的自匹配性，并用自匹配系数作为光学景象匹配区的选定准则，和以前提出的相关长度、独立像元数准则进行了分析对比，说明了这两个准则的实质。通过分析和实验得出了相对于相关匹配算法而言，自匹配系数准则效果更佳的结论，对于光学景象匹配区的选择具有一定的实用价值。
关键词：光学景象匹配；相关算法；选定准则

A Selection Criterion of Scene Matching Field Based on Image Self-Matching
MA Long（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College，Xi'an Shaanxi 710025,China）　
MIAO Dong（Xi'an Second Artillery Engineering College，Xi'an Shaanxi 710025,China）　
SHEN Xu-bang（Lishan Microelectronic Crop. Xi'an Shaanxi 710054，China）　

Abstract：The Image self-matching is defined, the self-matching coefficient has been used as a selection criterion of scene matching field. This selection criterion has been analyzed and contrasted with the previous criterion named correlation length and the number of independent image element. The essentials of the two criterions have been showed. Through analysis and experiment, a conclusion has been obtained that the selection criterion of self-matching is more effective to correlation-matching algorithm. It is valuable for the selection of scene matching field.
Keywords：Scene matching； Correlation algorithm； Selection criterion
